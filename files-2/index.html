Our Story

Planning, grounded in nature's patience.

About / Founder Photo images/about-photo.jpg · 1000×1250px

Fern & Current was founded on a simple belief: the best events feel inevitable, like water finding its path downhill. Nothing forced, nothing rushed — just every piece settling exactly where it belongs.

We bring meticulous planning and a calm, grounded presence to every gathering we touch, so you can stay present in the moment instead of managing the logistics behind it.

Every project starts with listening. We learn your story, your people, and your pace, then build a plan as considered and singular as the landscape it's set in.

Rooted in Detail

Timelines, budgets, and logistics are tracked with the same care as the centerpieces.

Calm Under Pressure

When something shifts on the day-of, you won't even notice — we already adjusted.

Designed to Feel Alive

We favor organic textures and natural materials over anything that feels staged.

Built Around You

No two plans look alike, because no two clients — or stories — are the same.
